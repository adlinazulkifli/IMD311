MY EXPERIENCE

High School Experience!

Talking about high school life, I had enjoyed my high school life to the fullest and had some of the best memories of my life. When I’ve in the senior year, I was moved to a new school which was close to my father’s office. At first, I was really afraid as all the people were new to me. But soon, I started enjoying my senior year life and made some fantastic friends. I loved everything about my new high school and participated in the events or programmes that the school held. Even I joined the school uniform bodies camping in my school and participated in many competitions that occurred in school.

One of the best things about high school life is that you get a new experience every day. In my high school life, along with studying, me and my friends enjoyed a lot of other things. We shared a lot of stories, planned a trip after the high school, had new experiences, and learned many new things. I never thought I would meet great friends like them all of a sudden. People say that high school is going to be the best things in your life and being that I’m a senior, I would have to agree. This last year as a student in high school has been awesome. My personality hasn’t changed, I’m still a carefree girl, just with a little more wisdom and a lot more strength.
